Over the last year, Burning Man Project has collaborated with Google on the plan to install six artworks in the new public Charleston East Plaza in Mountain View, California.

Through an international call for artists and a six-month art review and community feedback process, we first narrowed the submissions down to the 45 semi-finalists shown below, then ultimately to the six grantees.

The artworks will be fabricated in 2020 and installed in 2021. For those interested, there will be opportunities to help with some of these projects. Stay tuned for more details, as well as the opening celebration of Charleston East Plaza in 2021.
